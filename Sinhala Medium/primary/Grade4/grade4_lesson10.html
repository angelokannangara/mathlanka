Lesson 10: Time - Let's learn about clocks and calendars!

For Grade 4 Students (Ages 9-10) - Let's learn to read time, convert units, and solve time problems!

What we will learn today:

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Read time on **analog and digital clocks**.
  • Convert between **hours, minutes, and seconds**.
  • Calculate **duration** of events.
  • Solve **word problems** involving time.

Units of Time:

Time is measured in different units. Here are some important conversions:

Conversions:

1 minute = 60 seconds

1 hour = 60 minutes

1 day = 24 hours

1 week = 7 days

1 year = 12 months = 365 days (366 in a leap year)

Activity 1: Read the Time!

What time is shown on the clock? Enter in HH:MM format (e.g., 03:45).

Activity 2: Convert Time Units!

Convert the given time unit and enter the answer.

Exercises: Time Calculations

1. How many minutes are in 2 hours?

2. How many hours are in 3 days?

3. Convert 180 seconds to minutes.

4. Convert 240 minutes to hours.

Word Problems: Solve using Time

  1. A train journey starts at 8:00 AM and ends at 11:30 AM. How long is the journey?
  2. You spend 45 minutes on homework and 30 minutes reading. How much time do you spend in total?
