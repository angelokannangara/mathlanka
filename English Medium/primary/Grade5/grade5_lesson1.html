Lesson 1: Numbers - 1

For Grade 5 Students (Ages 10-11) - Let's explore numbers and their properties!

What we will learn today:

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Identify and write numbers up to 100,000.
  • Understand place value of digits in numbers.
  • Write numbers in expanded form.
  • Compare and order numbers.
