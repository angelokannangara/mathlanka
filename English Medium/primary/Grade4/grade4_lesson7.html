Lesson 7: Division - 1 - Let's learn to divide!

For Grade 4 Students (Ages 9-10) - Let's learn basic division and solve problems!

What we will learn today:

In this lesson, you will learn to:

  • Understand division as **sharing equally** or **repeated subtraction**.
  • Divide **two-digit numbers by single-digit numbers** with no remainder.
  • Solve **word problems** involving division.

What is Division?

Division is splitting a number into equal parts. It tells us how many groups there are or how many are in each group.

Example:

You have 12 cookies and want to share them equally among 3 friends.

Each friend gets $12 \div 3 = 4$ cookies.

Word Problems: Solve by Dividing

  1. You have 20 candies to share equally among 4 friends. How many candies does each friend get?
  2. A class has 30 students, and they need to form groups of 5. How many groups can they form?
  3. A gardener has 48 flowers to plant in 6 rows. If each row has the same number of flowers, how many flowers are in each row?
  4. A book has 90 pages. If you read 10 pages each day, how many days will it take to finish the book?
